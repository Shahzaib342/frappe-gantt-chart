Gantt Chart

A Gantt chart is a graphical representation of activity against time. A Gantt chart is a project management tool assisting in the planning and scheduling of projects of all sizes, although they are particularly useful for simplifying complex projects.

This repo will give you a ready made gannt chart with sample data. You can extend the chart with dynamic data and add more extended tooltip or you can handle different events based on your need. After customization you can embed it anywhere on your app.

Features

  • You just need to prepare a dynamic data and pass it to the chart object and it will do the rest.
  • It gives you two options clients and date. You can filter the data by clients with ability to show it by day, month or year. Ofcourse you can change clients to whatever other data based on your need.
  • It has the ability to add and customize tooltip.
  • It gives you the ability to add event listeners like click, on date change, on view change and redraw the chart based on any event.
  • Fully responsive
